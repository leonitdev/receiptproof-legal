Privacy Policy

App: ReceiptProof  •  Last updated: 11 February 2026

ReceiptProof (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and disclosed when you use the ReceiptProof mobile application (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Receipts and related data you upload (images, PDFs, text, purchase details).
  • Optional metadata you add (notes, tags, categories).
  • Account information (if you create an account), such as email address.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system version).
  • App usage data (features used, basic diagnostics) and crash logs.

This data is collected solely to ensure app functionality, security, and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the core functionality of the App.
  • Store, organize, and display your receipts.
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising or data brokerage.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored using reasonable security measures, including encryption where applicable.
  • We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information.
  • ReceiptProof does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Despite strong protections, no system is 100% secure. You use the App at your own risk.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and integrity of ReceiptProof and our users.
  • With trusted service providers strictly necessary to operate the App (for example, secure cloud storage), under confidentiality and security obligations.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

ReceiptProof may offer optional paid subscriptions.

  • Payments are processed by Apple via the App Store.
  • ReceiptProof does not collect or store your payment card details.
  • Subscription management and refunds are handled through your Apple ID settings.

6. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained only for as long as needed to provide the App and its features.
  • If you delete your account (if applicable) or remove content, that data may be permanently deleted, subject to technical and legal constraints.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to restrict or object to certain processing.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us (see Section 10).

8. Children’s Privacy

ReceiptProof is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective when posted on this page. Continued use of the App means acceptance of the updated policy.
