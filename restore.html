Restore Purchases Policy ReceiptProof ReceiptProof supports restoring purchases made through official app stores. How Restore Purchases Works Purchases are linked to your Apple ID or Google account Restoring purchases reactivates previously purchased subscriptions or features No additional payment is required When to Use Restore Purchases You reinstall the App You change devices Your subscription appears inactive after login Limitations Purchases cannot be transferred between accounts Restores are only available for purchases made on the same platform (iOS or Android) Refunds are handled exclusively by Apple or Google Troubleshooting If restore does not work: Ensure you are logged into the correct App Store account Check your active subscriptions in the App Store / Play Store Restart the App and try again If issues persist, contact: support@receiptproof.app
