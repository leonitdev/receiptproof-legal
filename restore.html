Restore Purchases

Product: ReceiptProof
Last updated: January 19, 2026

If you purchased ReceiptProof premium features via Apple App Store or Google Play, you can restore them without paying again.

How Restore Purchases Works

  • Purchases are linked to your Apple ID (iOS) or Google account (Android).
  • Restore re-checks your purchase status and re-enables entitlements on this device.
  • No additional payment is required.

When You Should Restore

  • You reinstalled the App
  • You changed devices
  • Premium features appear locked after sign-in

Limitations

  • Restores require using the same Store account used for purchase.
  • iOS purchases cannot be restored on Android and vice versa.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple/Google according to their policies.

Troubleshooting

  • Confirm you are signed into the correct Apple ID / Google account.
  • Verify the subscription is active in Store subscription settings.
  • Restart the App and try Restore again.
